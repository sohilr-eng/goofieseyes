What I do

The work, explored.

🌍

Travel Photography

I go to places most people never reach and document exactly what I find there. No tourist angles, no familiar frames — just raw, unfiltered documentation of the world as it actually exists off the map.

🌿

Landscape & Nature

Earth's drama, frozen in a single frame. From quiet golden hour fields to violent storm fronts — I'm there.

🚶

Street Photography

People doing their thing — unbothered, unposed, fully alive. I move through cities and neighbourhoods quietly, letting real moments happen and catching them when they do.

🎞

Documentary

Real light. Real moments. Long-form visual essays that bear witness to lives lived with extraordinary ordinariness.
